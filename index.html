Nordic winter notes

Real places to anchor a route.

  • Levi, Finland Finland's Lapland ski village pairs fell skiing with sauna culture, reindeer routes, and quiet Arctic evenings. Address: Hissitie 8, 99130 Levi, Finland.
  • Trysil, Norway Norway's large family-friendly ski area is shaped around mountain cabins, groomed forest pistes, and relaxed after-ski. Address: Velkomstvegen 10, 2420 Trysil, Norway.
  • Ruka, Finland A Kuusamo fell resort known for long winter light, ski-jump heritage, snowy spruce forests, and Finnish sauna rituals. Address: Rukatunturintie 9, 93830 Rukatunturi, Finland.
